# Phoenix Strategy: The Transmigrated Princess Who Ran Away with the Baby

## Story Synopsis

A modern girl named Su Nian accidentally transmigrates to ancient times, becoming an orphan with no one to rely on. By chance, she is rescued by the cold-faced Prince Xiao Yu, but unexpectedly, one night of folly results in a secret pregnancy.

One is a soul from the future; the other is a prince who holds power over the imperial court. When the truth of the pregnancy is revealed, a delicate emotion begins to sprout between them. However, the disparity in their status, layers of misunderstandings, political conspiracies, and schemes by love rivals...

She wants to escape but cannot break free from fate's bonds; he wants to keep her but doesn't know how to make her stay.

A love that spans a thousand years, a passionate romance of falling in love after marriage.

She ran away with the baby, and he chased his wife to the ends of the earth.

---

## Main Characters

### Female Lead: Su Nian
- A modern transmigrator, originally an ordinary white-collar worker in the 21st century
- Personality: Intelligent and independent, strong and enduring, soft-hearted inside
- Talents: Knows medicine, can cook, has modern ways of thinking
- Fate: Falls into a difficult situation after unexpectedly becoming pregnant

### Male Lead: Xiao Yu (Prince Yu)
- A prince of the current dynasty, cold-faced and cold-hearted, with illustrious military achievements
- Personality: Cold on the outside but warm inside, domineering and deeply affectionate, not good at expressing emotions
- Fate: Forced into political whirlpools, experiences misunderstandings and separation with the female lead

### Second Male Lead: Gu Yan
- A gentle and refined young master from a prestigious family
- Falls in love with the female lead at first sight, becomes her guardian
- Identity: Son of the Imperial Hospital's chief physician

### Villainess: Liu Ruoyan
- A candidate for the prince's consort
- Deeply scheming, calculating every step
- Targets the female lead at every turn

### Important Supporting Characters
- Mama Wang: Steward of the prince's residence, initially makes things difficult for the female lead but is later moved by her kindness
- Prince Xiao Yu's mother: Consort De, strongly opposes this marriage
- Xiao Cui: The female lead's maid, loyal and devoted
