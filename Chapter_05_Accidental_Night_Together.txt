## Chapter 5: Accidental Night Together

That night, Su Nian had a strange dream.

She dreamed she was in a dark place, with someone chasing her.

"Catch her! Don't let her get away!"

That voice was sinister, making her shiver all over.

She ran desperately, but couldn't run fast no matter what.

Suddenly, a hand grabbed her ankle.

She fell to the ground, looking back to see a dark figure slowly crawling toward her.

"Su Nian... Su Nian..."

That dark figure emitted a strange laugh, "Do you think you can escape?"

Su Nian suddenly woke up.

She gasped for breath, realizing she was covered in sweat.

Outside the window, the moonlight was hazy, and the night was deep.

Su Nian sat up and patted her chest.

Fortunately, it was just a dream.

She was about to lie down again when she suddenly heard a commotion outside.

"What's going on?" She put on her outer garment and walked to the window.

She saw firelight flickering in the courtyard, seemingly with many people.

Su Nian's heart tightened, and she quickly pushed open the door and walked out.

She saw Xiao Cui running toward her in a hurry: "Miss, it's bad! There's a fire!"

A fire?

Su Nian was shocked: "Where is the fire?"

"The East Wing! Not far from us!" Xiao Cui's face was pale with fear, "I heard someone deliberately set the fire. The fire is big and has already burned several rooms!"

Su Nian looked out to see the distant sky illuminated red by the fire, with thick smoke billowing.

If this fire spread, the entire residence would be in danger!

"Quick! Go help put out the fire!" Su Nian said, about to run out.

Xiao Cui grabbed her: "Miss, you can't go, it's too dangerous!"

"No!" Su Nian shook off her hand, "I have to see if I can help!"

Although she was a white-collar worker in her previous life, she wasn't someone who would just hide behind others. Besides, with such a big fire, if it spread to their side, they wouldn't be able to escape either.

Su Nian ignored Xiao Cui's obstruction and ran out of the courtyard.

Along the way, she saw many servants carrying water to fight the fire, in complete chaos.

The fire was indeed large, with raging flames devouring the houses, making crackling sounds.

Su Nian wanted to help but was choked by the thick smoke and coughed repeatedly.

"Little girl, it's dangerous here, step back!" A guard stopped her.

Su Nian was about to speak when she suddenly slipped and fell backward—

Just when she thought she was going to fall, a hand timely supported her.

"Careful."

That voice was deep and pleasant, carrying a familiar scent.

Su Nian looked up and met Xiao Yu's gaze.

He was wearing a black outfit tonight, standing tall and straight like a pine. The firelight shone on his face, illuminating his deep contours.

In the firelight, his eyes were a bit softer than usual.

"Your Highness..." Su Nian looked at him blankly.

Xiao Yu helped her stand steady and frowned: "Why did you come out? It's dangerous here."

"I wanted to help put out the fire..."

"Help with what?" Xiao Yu said unhappily, "You can't even lift water, what trouble are you adding?"

Su Nian was choked by him, not knowing what to say.

Xiao Yu sighed, pulled her hand, and walked backward: "Come with this prince, it's not safe here."

He held her hand, not with much force, but not allowing refusal.

Su Nian was led forward by him, her heartbeat inexplicably quickening.

She subconsciously wanted to pull her hand back, but found Xiao Yu holding tighter.

"Don't move."

Su Nian had to obediently follow him.

The two passed through the chaotic crowd and came to a relatively safe place.

Xiao Yu let go of her hand and looked at her with a complex expression.

"You almost fell just now. Do you know what would have happened if this prince hadn't been there?"

Su Nian shook her head.

Xiao Yu stared at her for a long time, then suddenly sighed: "Forget it, as long as you're fine."

Su Nian looked at the concern in his eyes, and a warm current welled up in her heart.

"Your Highness, you're so good to me." She blurted out.

Xiao Yu was stunned, then averted his gaze: "This prince just doesn't want the person this prince saved to have an accident."

With that, he turned and left, disappearing into the night.

Su Nian watched his departing figure, the corners of her mouth involuntarily curving up.

This cold-faced prince actually had quite a soft heart.

That night, Su Nian spent in Xiao Yu's study.

After the fire was brought under control, it was almost dawn. Xiao Cui let her rest on the soft couch in the study. She was too tired and fell asleep as soon as her head hit the pillow.

When she woke up the next morning, she found a thin blanket covering her.

On the desk, there was still a bowl of steaming hot porridge and a few small dishes.

Next to it was a note with Xiao Yu's handwriting:

"Finish breakfast before you leave."

Su Nian looked at that note, her heart melting.

This man was really good at flirting.

But then again, there was no possibility between them.

She was a transmigrator and would have to return to modern times sooner or later. Moreover, she still had mysteries on her and could be in danger at any time.

She couldn't implicate him.

Su Nian sighed, carefully folded that note, and put it in her sleeve.

Then she picked up the porridge bowl and drank it sip by sip.

No matter what, she would get through the days ahead first.

As for the rest, she'd talk about it later.
