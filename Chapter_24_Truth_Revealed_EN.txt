## Chapter 24: Truth Revealed

After returning to the Capital City, Xiao Yu did something momentous.

He petitioned the Emperor for a decree to take Su Nian as his Princess Consort.

The Emperor hesitated.

Su Nian was, after all, a woman of unknown origins — no family name, no background to speak of.

For the Regent Prince to take such a woman as his primary wife would surely invite criticism.

But Xiao Yu stood firm.

He said that though Su Nian was a transmigrator, she was kind-hearted and immensely talented. She had saved him, and she had saved this nation. A woman like her was worth devoting his entire life to protecting.

The Emperor was moved and finally relented.

But before the formal wedding, there was one more thing Xiao Yu needed to do.

He uncovered the secret of Su Nian's origins.

It turned out that the original owner of Su Nian's body was a descendant of the former dynasty's princess.

When the old dynasty had fallen, the princess had narrowly escaped, changing her name and living in hiding among the common people. The mother of this body was the princess's own great-granddaughter.

So, strictly speaking, Su Nian — or rather, the original owner of the body — carried the royal bloodline of the former dynasty.

But the old dynasty had fallen decades ago; her identity no longer held any political significance.

Xiao Yu, however, cared nothing about that.

He simply wanted Su Nian to have a proper identity, so that their marriage would be beyond reproach.

When Su Nian learned of this, she was filled with deep emotion.

She had never imagined that the origins of this body were so complex.

But she cared nothing for any princess's bloodline. She was simply grateful to have met Xiao Yu, and to be with him.
