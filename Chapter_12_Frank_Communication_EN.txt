## Chapter 12: Frank Communication

In the days that followed, Xiao Yu came to visit Su Nian every day.

Sometimes in the morning, sometimes in the evening, and sometimes just a fleeting glance before he left.

He did not say much, but each time he came, he brought something she liked to eat.

Sometimes pastries, sometimes fruit, sometimes bird's nest bestowed by the palace.

Su Nian said nothing, but her heart was warm.

Xiao Cui watched from the side and could not resist teasing her. "Miss, His Highness is truly kind to you."

Su Nian shot her a disapproving look. "Don't talk nonsense. His Highness is only concerned about the child."

"That's not true at all." Xiao Cui covered her mouth, giggling. "The way His Highness looks at you is different now. I think he must have feelings for you."

Su Nian's face flushed.

She dared not admit it, but she had felt the same way.

Xiao Yu's attitude toward her had noticeably softened. Sometimes he would even chat with her and ask about the modern world.

She had not hidden the fact that she was a transmigrator — she had told him everything in full detail.

Xiao Yu listened in silence for a long time.

"You're saying you come from a thousand years in the future?"

Su Nian nodded. "I know it sounds incredible. But it is the truth."

Xiao Yu looked at her. "Then why did you appear here?"

Su Nian shook her head. "I don't know either. That night I was working late, and suddenly everything went dark. When I woke up, I was in this world."

Xiao Yu frowned. "What is the world like over there?"

Su Nian thought for a moment and began telling him about modern life.

She spoke of cars, airplanes, television, mobile phones... of skyscrapers, the internet, artificial intelligence...

Xiao Yu listened in stunned amazement.

"The world a thousand years from now is truly that miraculous?"

Su Nian nodded. "Yes. Technology has advanced so rapidly. There are things you could not even imagine."

Xiao Yu looked at her, a complex emotion flickering in his eyes.

"Then... do you want to go back?"

Su Nian fell silent for a moment.

Go back? Of course she wanted to.

She wanted to return to the modern world, to resume her life, to see her friends and colleagues, to order takeout, drink bubble tea, scroll through her phone...

But now...

"I don't know," she said honestly. "I originally wanted to go back. But now..."

She placed a hand on her belly.

Now she was carrying his child. How could she leave and go back alone?

Xiao Yu understood her meaning.

He did not speak. He simply looked at her in silence.

That gaze was deep and complex — she could not read his thoughts.

After a long while, he spoke. "If one day you could go back — would you?"

Su Nian looked at him and thought carefully.

"I don't know," she said. "Perhaps I would. Perhaps I wouldn't."

Xiao Yu frowned. "Why?"

Su Nian smiled. "Because there are people here I care about."

Xiao Yu's gaze flickered.

He looked at her, and she looked at him.

Their eyes met, and the air seemed to still.

Su Nian's face grew redder and redder, her heartbeat faster and faster.

Had she just said too much?

How could she say such a thing in front of Xiao Yu?

Just as she was about to explain, Xiao Yu suddenly spoke:

"The person you care about — is it me?"

Su Nian froze.

How could he ask so directly?

She wanted to deny it, but the words would not come.

Because... yes.

The person she cared about was him.

From that night onward, her heart had already belonged to him.

Xiao Yu looked at her, waiting for her answer.

Su Nian took a deep breath and finally nodded.

"Yes," she said. "The person I care about is you."

A glimmer of joy flashed in Xiao Yu's eyes.

He stepped forward and took her hand.

"Su Nian, I also..."

Before he could finish, a commotion erupted outside the door.

"Your Highness! Your Highness!" The guard's voice was urgent. "Someone has come from the palace! His Majesty summons you to the palace immediately!"

Xiao Yu's expression darkened.

He released Su Nian's hand. "I must go deal with this. Rest well."

Su Nian nodded. "Be careful, Your Highness."

Xiao Yu gave her one last look, then turned and left.

Su Nian watched his retreating figure, her heart caught between sweetness and worry.

What had he been about to say? Was he going to say... that he cared about her too?

She could not be certain.

But she knew that the relationship between them had changed.
