# *Phoenix's Scheme: The Transmigrated Princess Consort Runs Away with Child*

## Synopsis

A modern girl named Su Nian accidentally transmigrates to ancient times, becoming a helpless orphan girl. Through a twist of fate, she is rescued by the cold-faced Prince Xiao Yu — but one reckless night leaves her secretly carrying his child.

One is a soul from the future; the other, a prince who wields immense power over the court. When the truth of her pregnancy is revealed, a delicate emotion begins to blossom between them. Yet the gulf in their stations, layers of misunderstanding, political conspiracies, and the schemes of a rival in love threaten to tear them apart...

She longs to flee but cannot escape the bonds of destiny. He wishes her to stay but knows not how to make her remain.

A love that spans a thousand years — a tale of anguish and devotion, of marriage before love.

She ran away carrying his child; now he must chase his wife through a crucible of regret.

---

## Main Characters

### Female Lead: Su Nian
- A transmigrator from the modern era, formerly an ordinary white-collar worker of the 21st century
- Personality: intelligent and independent, resilient and forbearing, with a tender heart
- Skills: knowledgeable in medicine, an excellent cook, and equipped with a modern mindset
- Fate: plunged into dire straits after an unexpected pregnancy

### Male Lead: Xiao Yu (Prince Yu)
- A prince of the realm, cold in demeanor and heart, with illustrious military achievements
- Personality: cold on the outside but warm within, commanding yet deeply devoted, awkward at expressing his feelings
- Fate: drawn into a political maelstrom, separated from the female lead through misunderstanding

### Second Male Lead: Gu Yan
- A gentleman of noble lineage, gentle as jade
- Falls in love with the female lead at first sight and becomes her protector
- Identity: son of the Chief Physician of the Imperial Medical Court

### Antagonist: Liu Ru Yan
- A candidate for the position of Concubine in the prince's household
- Deeply scheming, calculating at every step
- Constantly targets the female lead

### Key Supporting Characters
- Mammy Wang: Steward of Prince Yu's Residence; initially makes things difficult for the female lead but is later moved by her sincerity
- Prince Yu's Mother: Consort De, who vehemently opposes the match
- Xiao Cui: The female lead's personal maid, fiercely loyal
