# "Phoenix Strategy: The Transmigrated Princess Who Ran Away with the Baby"

> A love spanning thousands of years, a deep and torturous romance of marriage before love.
> 
> She ran away with the baby, and he chased her to the ends of the earth.

---

## 📖 Synopsis

"Phoenix Strategy: The Transmigrated Princess Who Ran Away with the Baby" is an ancient time-travel romance novel that tells the tumultuous love story between Su Nian, a modern girl who accidentally transmigrates to ancient times, and Xiao Yu, a cold-faced prince.

**Core Concept**: Pregnancy before love + Deep and torturous romance + Reunion after separation

---

## 👥 Main Characters

### Female Lead: Su Nian
- **Identity**: Modern transmigrator, ordinary white-collar worker from the 21st century
- **Personality**: Intelligent and independent, strong and enduring, soft-hearted
- **Skills**: Knowledge of medicine, good at cooking, modern way of thinking
- **Fate**: Accidentally transmigrates and becomes pregnant, falling into difficult circumstances

### Male Lead: Xiao Yu (Prince Yu)
- **Identity**: Prince of the dynasty, God of War of Great Zhou
- **Personality**: Cold outside but warm inside, domineering and deeply affectionate, not good at expressing emotions
- **Fate**: Forced into political whirlpools, experiences misunderstandings and separation with the female lead

### Second Male Lead: Gu Yan / Yelü Qi
- **Identity**: Prince of the Northern Di Kingdom, disguised as the son of the Imperial Medical Court's judge
- **Characteristics**: Gentle and jade-like appearance hiding deep scheming
- **Role**: Drives plot development, creates conflicts

### Female Antagonist: Liu Ru Yan
- **Identity**: Concubine of the Prince's residence
- **Personality**: Deeply scheming, calculating every step
- **Actions**: Targets the female lead everywhere, spreads rumors
