## Chapter 18: Rival Schemes

That night, Su Nian did not sleep at all.

She thought about many things.

Was Xiao Yu's kindness to her genuine?

Or was it only because of the child she carried?

If one day the child was born, would he still treat her well?

She did not know.

She only knew that she was afraid.

Afraid that all of this was just a dream. Afraid that Xiao Yu was merely using her. Afraid that she would give everything, only to end up with nothing.

At dawn, she finally made her decision.

Since he was going to send her away, she would leave on her own terms.

Rather than being driven out, she would depart with dignity.

She packed her belongings, left a letter, and quietly slipped out of the residence.

The letter read:

"Xiao Yu, I have left. I will take good care of the child. You need not worry."

That was all. No farewell, no explanation.

She was afraid that if she wrote even one more word, she would not be able to hold back her tears.

---

By the time Xiao Yu discovered Su Nian was gone, it was already noon.

He rushed into her room and saw the empty space. He stood there, stunned.

"Where is she?" He grabbed Xiao Cui and demanded harshly, "Where is Su Nian?"

Xiao Cui was sobbing. "Miss... Miss has gone! She left a letter and just left!"

Xiao Yu snatched the letter. After reading it, his face turned livid.

"Damn it!" He crumpled the letter into a ball. "Who told her I was going to send her away?!"

Xiao Cui stammered through her tears. "It... it was Concubine Liu. Concubine Liu came yesterday and said... said something about His Majesty's marriage decree, and that His Highness was going to send Miss away..."

A murderous glint flashed in Xiao Yu's eyes.

Liu Ru Yan!

Her again!

He spun around, about to go confront Liu Ru Yan, but the steward stopped him.

"Your Highness, please calm yourself! The most important thing right now is to find Miss Su! Miss Su is pregnant — it is far too dangerous for her to be out there alone!"

Xiao Yu forced himself to calm down.

The steward was right. Now was not the time to assign blame.

He took a deep breath and issued his orders. "Send men out to search immediately! Turn the entire Capital City upside down if you must — but find her!"

"Yes!"

Xiao Yu stood in the empty room, feeling as though something had been hollowed out of his chest.

Su Nian...

How could she do this?

He had told her he would never let her suffer. He had told her he liked her.

Why did she not trust him?

Why did she leave?

He did not know.

He only knew that he could not lose her.
