## Chapter 4: Secretly Developing Feelings

In the following days, Su Nian copied account books in the study every day.

Xiao Cui accompanied her by her side, helping her grind ink and serve tea.

Gradually, Su Nian gained some understanding of the writing in this world.

The writing used in this world was similar to traditional Chinese characters; most of it she could recognize, but the writing style was somewhat different. Fortunately, she had practiced calligraphy in her previous life and could manage both hard pen and brush writing, so she could barely cope.

In the process of copying the account books, she also learned some information about this world.

The economic system, tax policy, and commercial trade of the Great Zhou Dynasty... these were all reflected in the account books.

Su Nian sighed while copying: It wasn't easy being an accountant in ancient times either, having to write so many characters, and all with a brush.

This day, she was concentrating on copying when footsteps suddenly came from the doorway.

She looked up to see Xiao Yu standing in the doorway, looking at her.

"Your Highness?" Su Nian quickly stood up to salute, "When did Your Highness arrive?"

"Just arrived." Xiao Yu walked into the study and picked up the account books she had copied to look through.

Su Nian felt a bit nervous, not knowing if he would dislike her copying.

After a moment, Xiao Yu put down the account books: "Your handwriting is better than this prince imagined."

Su Nian breathed a sigh of relief: "Your Highness flatters me. This commoner is just a bit more careful."

Xiao Yu didn't speak, his gaze lingering on her face for a moment.

Su Nian felt a bit uncomfortable under his gaze and lowered her eyes.

The atmosphere was momentarily subtle.

Just then, Su Nian's stomach suddenly growled.

Her face turned red, wishing she could find a crack in the ground to crawl into.

She had been busy copying books at noon and forgot to eat. Now she was making a fool of herself in front of the prince!

Xiao Yu raised an eyebrow, a look of surprise flashing in his eyes.

"Hungry?"

Su Nian nodded awkwardly: "Yes... this commoner forgot to have lunch."

Xiao Yu was silent for a moment, then turned to instruct the guard at the door: "Go have the kitchen send some refreshments."

"Yes."

Su Nian was stunned, not expecting him to take the initiative to order food for her.

A moment later, the guard brought a plate of refreshments. Exquisite osmanthus cakes, emitting an enticing aroma.

"Eat." Xiao Yu said indifferently.

Su Nian hesitated for a moment, but still reached out to pick up a piece of osmanthus cake.

Taking a bite, it was sweet but not cloying, melting in her mouth.

Delicious!

Her eyes lit up, and she couldn't help but eat another piece.

Xiao Yu watched her eating with relish, a barely noticeable smile flashing in his eyes.

"You have quite good table manners."

Su Nian was stunned, looking up just in time to meet his gaze.

Their eyes met, and the distance between them was very close.

She could even see the lines at the bottom of his eyes and smell the faint scent of sandalwood on him.

Su Nian's heart skipped a beat.

She quickly averted her gaze: "Your Highness is joking. This commoner is just hungry."

Xiao Yu didn't speak, just quietly looked at her.

That gaze was unfathomable, making it impossible to guess what he was thinking.

Su Nian felt flustered under his gaze and was about to say something when a report suddenly came from outside the door:

"Your Highness, Mama Cui from Consort De's side has arrived!"

Xiao Yu's brow furrowed slightly, withdrawing his gaze: "Let her in."

A woman in her forties walked in and saluted: "This servant pays respects to Your Highness. Your Highness, Consort De wants you to enter the palace tomorrow, saying there is an important matter to discuss."

"Understood." Xiao Yu responded indifferently.

Mama Cui's gaze lingered on Su Nian for a moment, hesitating to speak.

Xiao Yu frowned: "Is there something else?"

"The Consort said..." Mama Cui hesitated, "The Consort said that a woman of unknown origins has come to the residence, and wants Your Highness to be more careful. It's best to send her away as soon as possible, to avoid gossip."

Su Nian heard this and felt somewhat uncomfortable.

Although she knew she was a trouble, being disliked to her face was still a bit unpleasant.

Xiao Yu's voice turned cold: "Mother's news is quite well-informed."

Mama Cui's face changed slightly: "The Consort is just concerned about Your Highness..."

"This prince's affairs are not for Mother to worry about." Xiao Yu interrupted her, "Go back and tell Mother that this prince will enter the palace tomorrow."

"Yes." Mama Cui didn't dare to say more and saluted to leave.

Only Su Nian and Xiao Yu were left in the study.

The atmosphere was momentarily awkward.

Su Nian didn't know what to say, so she had to lower her head and continue copying books.

But her mind was completely not on the account books.

Consort De was obviously her mother-in-law. Although not biological, she was still a member of the imperial family. It was indeed inappropriate for her to live in the residence like this.

"What are you thinking about?" Xiao Yu's voice suddenly sounded.

Su Nian looked up and said after some consideration: "This commoner is thinking, perhaps I should leave the residence to avoid causing trouble for Your Highness."

Xiao Yu looked at her: "You want to leave?"

Su Nian shook her head: "This commoner has nowhere to go for the time being, and those people hunting this commoner haven't been cleared up yet. This commoner wants to wait until things are clear before making plans."

Xiao Yu was silent for a moment: "Then stay."

Su Nian was stunned: "Your Highness?"

Xiao Yu stood up: "The person this prince saved is not for others to gossip about."

With that, he strode away.

Su Nian watched his departing figure, a feeling she couldn't quite describe welling up in her heart.

This man, obviously cold-faced and cold-hearted, always helped her at critical moments.

She knew this was wrong. She was a transmigrator and would have to leave here sooner or later.

But at this moment, her heart still couldn't help but flutter.
