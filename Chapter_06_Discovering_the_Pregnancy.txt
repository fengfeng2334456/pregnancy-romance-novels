## Chapter 6: Discovering the Pregnancy

After that day, Su Nian continued copying books in the study.

Xiao Yu came more frequently than before, sometimes even staying with her for an entire afternoon.

He still didn't talk much, but when she was tired from copying, he would have someone send refreshments. When the weather changed, he would instruct the servants to bring her an extra garment.

Su Nian didn't say anything, but her heart was warm.

She knew this was wrong. Xiao Yu was a prince, with a concubine, and would have a wife in the future. And she was just a woman of unknown origins.

But she couldn't control her heart.

Whenever she saw him appear, her eyes would involuntarily follow him. Whenever she heard his voice, her heartbeat would unconsciously quicken.

She seemed... to have fallen in love with him.

Su Nian shook her head, shaking off these thoughts.

No, she couldn't continue like this. She had to figure out the origins of those men in black as soon as possible, then leave the residence.

But some things weren't something she could control just by wanting to.

Like... her body.

Recently, she had been feeling something was off.

She would feel nauseous and want to vomit in the morning, with no appetite for anything. Her body was also getting more easily tired, always wanting to sleep.

She thought it was heatstroke from the hot weather. She went to see a doctor, and the doctor also said there was no major problem.

But Xiao Cui felt something was wrong: "Miss, your symptoms... could it be that you're expecting?"

Su Nian was stunned: "Expecting what?"

Xiao Cui's face turned red: "Just... that kind of symptom. This servant previously served a lady who was pregnant, and she was like this too, vomiting and tired..."

Su Nian's heart skipped a beat.

Pregnant? No way?

She indeed hadn't had her period for two months. But she thought it was because her body hadn't adapted after transmigrating, or stress-induced amenorrhea.

But thinking about it now...

Her face changed.

Two months ago, when she had just transmigrated, she and Xiao Yu had...

That night she had a high fever, burning deliriously. Xiao Yu stayed by her side without rest, and in the middle of the night when she was feverish and confused, she mistook Xiao Yu for her boyfriend, and the two of them...

The next morning when she woke up, she wished she could find a crack in the ground to crawl into. Xiao Yu was also awkward and said he would take responsibility.

But afterward, the two tacitly never mentioned it again, both acting as if nothing had happened.

Could it be... she got pregnant in one try?

The more Su Nian thought about it, the more panicked she became, and she quickly had Xiao Cui call a doctor.

After the doctor arrived, he carefully took her pulse.

A moment later, the doctor stood up and looked at her with a complex expression.

"Miss," he lowered his voice, "you have... a pregnancy pulse."

Su Nian felt her mind go blank.

Pregnancy pulse. It meant she was pregnant.

She was really pregnant.

Pregnant with Xiao Yu's child.

"Old... Old Sir," she spoke with difficulty, "are you sure?"

The old doctor stroked his beard: "This old man has been practicing medicine for thirty years, I won't make a mistake. Miss is indeed pregnant, about two months along."

Two months. The timing matched.

Su Nian's mind was in chaos.

She had only transmigrated to this world for two months, and she was going to be a mother?

This was too fast, wasn't it?

"Miss, what do you plan to do with this child?" the old doctor asked cautiously.

Su Nian came back to her senses and took a deep breath: "Doctor, can you keep this secret? I don't want too many people to know for now."

The old doctor hesitated: "Miss, this can't be hidden. The prince will find out sooner or later..."

"I know." Su Nian said, "But please, Doctor, don't spread it for now. Let me think it through first."

The old doctor nodded: "Alright, Miss be careful. The first three months the pregnancy is unstable, you need to rest more and not overwork."

"Thank you, Doctor."

Su Nian sent the doctor off and sat alone in the room in a daze.

She was pregnant.

Pregnant with Xiao Yu's child.

This news was like a bolt from the blue to her.

What should she do?

Tell Xiao Yu? What would he think? Would he want this child? Would he marry her?

Or hide it? But could it be hidden? It would be discovered sooner or later.

Or... secretly abort it?

As soon as this thought emerged, she immediately rejected it.

No. This was a life. It was her and Xiao Yu's child. She had no right to deprive him of his life.

But if she gave birth? An unmarried woman giving birth to a child of unknown origins, in this world she would be pointed at and gossiped about.

Even if Xiao Yu was willing to take responsibility, would Consort De agree? Would Concubine Liu let it go?

And those men in black hunting her, what would they do if they knew she was pregnant?

A series of questions swirled in her mind.

Su Nian rubbed her temples, feeling like her head was about to explode.

She needed to calm down. Think carefully about what to do next.
