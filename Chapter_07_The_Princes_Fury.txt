## Chapter 7: The Prince's Fury

Before Su Nian could figure out what to do, trouble came knocking first.

This day, she was basking in the sun in the courtyard when Xiao Yu suddenly arrived.

His face looked terrible, dark and gloomy like the sky before a storm.

Su Nian's heart skipped a beat: "Your Highness? What's wrong?"

Xiao Yu didn't speak, just waved his hand to dismiss the servants.

Xiao Cui hesitated for a moment, but still sensibly retreated.

Only the two of them remained in the courtyard.

Xiao Yu stared at Su Nian for a long time before suddenly opening his mouth: "This prince asks you one thing, answer honestly."

Su Nian felt nervous under his gaze: "Your Highness, please ask."

"You've been unwell recently, what's going on?"

Su Nian's heart tightened. How did he know?

"Just... a bit uncomfortable, nothing serious."

Xiao Yu took a step closer: "The doctor examined you? What did the doctor say?"

Su Nian took a step back, not knowing whether to tell the truth.

Xiao Yu saw her silence, a complex look flashing in his eyes: "This prince has already asked the doctor."

Su Nian's face changed.

Xiao Yu took a deep breath, his voice cold as ice: "You're pregnant?"

It wasn't a question, it was a statement.

Su Nian knew she couldn't hide it anymore, so she nodded: "Yes."

As soon as the words fell, a loud slap hit her face.

She was slapped so hard she staggered, almost falling.

Su Nian covered her face, looking at Xiao Yu in disbelief.

He actually hit her?

Xiao Yu's hand was still suspended in mid-air, clearly also somewhat surprised by his own action. But soon, his face resumed a cold expression.

"This prince asks you, whose child is it?"

Su Nian was stunned.

He was asking whose child it was?

Could he think...

"Your Highness," she spoke with difficulty, "the child is yours."

Xiao Yu sneered: "Mine? You certainly know how to climb up! This prince is the dignified Prince Yu, how could I have relations with a woman of unknown origins like you?"

Su Nian's heart felt like it had been stabbed.

"You think this prince doesn't know?" Xiao Yu's voice carried suppressed anger, "That night, you took advantage of this prince taking care of you to seduce this prince! This prince thought you were a decent woman, but I didn't expect you to be so shameless!"

Su Nian's face was pale.

That night... that night she was delirious with fever, and Xiao Yu also took the initiative. How could he say that about her?

"Your Highness, you've misunderstood, that day was—"

"Enough!" Xiao Yu interrupted her, "You think this prince will believe your nonsense? You're just trying to use the child to climb up! Unfortunately, you've miscalculated!"

Su Nian looked at him, her eyes gradually reddening.

Was this the man she liked?

She thought although he was cold-faced and cold-hearted, he was kind inside. She thought although he didn't talk much, he had her in his heart.

But the result?

He didn't believe her at all. From beginning to end, he treated her as a woman trying to climb up to power.

"You think this prince doesn't know?" Xiao Yu sneered, "There's something wrong with your body, your menstruation is irregular, and it's difficult to get pregnant. You're trying to take this opportunity to latch onto this prince and marry into the residence, aren't you?"

Su Nian was stunned.

There was something wrong with her body? This body... difficult to conceive?

No, she didn't know about the original owner's body. Her own menstruation had always been normal.

"The doctor has examined," Xiao Yu's voice was cold as ice, "you have a cold constitution from birth, difficult to conceive. This pregnancy is because you used some folk remedy, right?"

Su Nian opened her mouth, wanting to say something, but found she had no way to defend herself.

She had only transmigrated for two months and indeed didn't know much about this body. She didn't even know if there was something wrong with the original owner's body.

But how could Xiao Yu think of her like this?

He was her savior, and she did have feelings for him. But she had never thought of using a child to climb up!

"Your Highness," her voice was hoarse, "the child really is yours. If you don't believe it, we can do a blood test."

"Blood test?" Xiao Yu sneered, "You think this prince would want a child of unknown origins?"

Su Nian's heart was completely cold.

He meant... he didn't want this child?

"This prince will give you some silver, you leave the residence, and from now on you have nothing to do with this prince."

Xiao Yu's words were like a bucket of cold water, drenching her thoroughly.

Su Nian looked at him, tears finally unable to hold back.

"Your Highness, how can you do this?" Her voice carried a sob, "The child is innocent. Even if you don't acknowledge me, you can't not want the child!"

A complex look flashed in Xiao Yu's eyes, but was soon replaced by coldness.

"This prince's bloodline cannot be left outside." He said, "This child, this prince will keep. But you, must go."

Su Nian was stunned.

Keep the child, but not the mother?

What kind of logic was this?

She was the child's mother, how could he separate them?

"No," Su Nian shook her head, "I'm not leaving. This child, I will raise myself."

Xiao Yu frowned: "You're an orphan, what do you have to raise a child with?"

"I'll find a way." Su Nian wiped her tears, "No matter how hard or difficult, I won't give up my child."

Xiao Yu stared at her for a long time, a barely noticeable emotion flashing in his eyes.

After a long time, he turned and left.

When he reached the door, he stopped: "In three days, leave the residence. This prince will arrange for someone to send you off."

With that, he strode away without looking back.

Su Nian watched his departing figure, tears flowing uncontrollably.

This was the man she fell in love with.

Cold-blooded, selfish, completely disregarding her feelings.

She was truly blind to have fallen in love with him.

But the child...

She touched her belly, tears flowing even more fiercely.

Sorry, child. It's mother's fault.

Your father doesn't want you, but mother will love you well.
